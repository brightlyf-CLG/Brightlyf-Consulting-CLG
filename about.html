About Brightlyf Consulting

Transforming Challenges into Opportunities for Growth

Who We Are

Brightlyf Consulting is a Kenyan consultancy specializing in personal development, education consulting, behavioural intervention, and rehabilitation support. We partner with schools, churches, youth organizations, community groups, and institutions seeking sustainable solutions to behavioural, developmental, and leadership challenges.

Our work is driven by the belief that behavioural challenges, emotional distress, leadership gaps, and social risks can be addressed through early intervention, structured support systems, and purposeful development pathways.

Why We Exist

Institutions today face increasing challenges including indiscipline, substance abuse, emotional distress, delinquency, harmful peer influence, leadership deficits, and behavioural crises.

Traditional approaches often focus on responding after problems occur. Brightlyf Consulting exists to help institutions identify risks early, intervene effectively, and create environments where individuals can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Approach

We deliver practical, evidence-informed interventions through our R.I.S.E.+R Framework:

  • R – Resilience Building
  • I – Intervention & Early Response
  • S – Stability & Behavioural Restructuring
  • E – Empowerment & Life Pathway Development
  • R – Risk Intelligence & Safety Monitoring

Who We Serve

  • Schools and Educational Institutions
  • Churches and Faith-Based Organizations
  • Youth and Community Organizations
  • Parents and Families
  • Leadership Development Programs
  • Rehabilitation and Reintegration Initiatives

Our Mission

To empower individuals and institutions through behavioural intervention, personal development, leadership formation, rehabilitation support, and sustainable growth pathways that create lasting positive impact.

Our Vision

To become a leading consultancy in Africa for behavioural transformation, personal development, and institutional capacity building, helping create safer, stronger, and more purpose-driven communities.

Our Founder

Brightlyf Consulting was founded by a multidisciplinary practitioner with training in Economics and Clinical Psychology and extensive experience in mentorship, behavioural intervention, leadership development, and youth empowerment. The founder has engaged more than 30 institutions since 2019, served in senior leadership roles, and leads both Thika Warriors Sports Club and Sunrise Success Entrepreneurship Forum.
